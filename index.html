Over 6.5+ years, I’ve shaped products at DoorDash, Apple, Fleetsmith, Massdrop, and other startups. I'm drawn to the problems most people overlook and making tools that feel effortless and crafted with care—with a hint of play.

Outside of work, I find the same satisfaction in a perfectly dialed in light roast pour over.
